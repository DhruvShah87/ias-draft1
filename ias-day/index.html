We cordially invite you to IAS DA-IICT Day celebration

About

IEEE IAS DA-IICT Student Branch Chapter was established on 15th of June and it has grown ever since then, therefore we decided to name it as "IAS DA-IICT Day". On this day, all the IEEE members across various branches will gather and celebrate, explore, learn and communicate with each other.

The event will begin with informative talks by some eminent personalities. It will be followed by some networking activities, photoshoot and a tour of the acclaimed GIFT City after Lunch Break.

The event will be a wonderful opportunity for networking and communication.

Venue

The event will be held in Gift City, Gandhinagar. Gujarat International Finance Tec-City (GIFT City) is India's first operational smart city and international financial services center. Various scientific techniques, internal workings of the city and current and future projects in detail will be explained during the tour.

Invited Speakers

Ms. Megha Tak

"Ms. Megha Tak is the Founder-Chairperson of IEEE IAS DA-IICT Student Branch Chapter. She is the current President of IEEE Young Professional. She's been working with IEEE since 2009, being IAS Student Branch Chair in 2012, and CMD Committee Member in 2013."

Dr. Anita Basalingappa

"Dr. Anita Basalingappa is a professor of Marketing at MICA. She holds a BSc (Honours) degree in Mathematics, an MBA in Marketing and a Ph.D. in Customer Relationship Marketing (CRM). Her current areas of interest include marketing and competition, social media behavior, marketing decision making, relationship marketing in rural retail, clothing as communication and CSR. She would be discussing the topic "How to market yourself?"."


Mr. Braj Bhushan Mishra

"Mr. Braj Bhushan Mishra is the general manager and head of Engineering and Fleet Management at ALSTOM Projects India Ltd. Having extensive experience in power generation and product design specifications he works as a steam turbine engineer. Using his knowledge of reverse engineering, he has helped in the development of various innovations in the company. He will speak about how his industry employs turbine technology and will discuss some technological marvels which we forget to value in our daily lives."

Mr. Sanjeev Agarwal

"Mr. Sanjeev Agarwal co-owner at Brainbow Academy of Multiple Intelligence, holds an MBA with great experience in Marketing and HR. He led the International marketing division as a Director in Jindal Worldwide Ltd. and was the Chief Manager at The Arvind Mills Ltd. He has a history of achieving illustrious business records in all his corporate assignments."
